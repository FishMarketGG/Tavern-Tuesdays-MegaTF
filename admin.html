📋 HOW IT WORKS

1. Load your existing stats.json first.
2. Upload your Frag Log and Player Log for the session.
3. Each player counts as 1 join per session upload, no matter how many times they connected/dropped.
4. Players named player, unnamed, or blank are ignored entirely.
5. Stats accumulate each week. At month end, click Award Monthly MVPs to lock in that month's winners.
6. Download the new stats.json and commit it to data/stats.json in your GitHub repo.
Frag log format — backslash-delimited, one kill per line:
\FishMarket[FF]\Aberrant\

Player log format — bracket timestamp + backslash fields:
[Thu Mar 26, 16:07:08 2026].[1] connect\FishMarket[FF]\1\...
